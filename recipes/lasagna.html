Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delightful recipe was passed to me by my Nona Rosalia, that I will always love. I will never forget how she used to make the tomato puree from scrach and grow her own basil.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pancetta
  • 500 ground pork and veal
  • 5 gloves of garlic
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 1 big onion
  • fresh egg pasta sheets

Steps

  1. finely chop the onion, garlic and celery/li>
  2. fry the pancetta in a little splash of extra virgin olive oil
  3. take out the pancetta and add onions, garlic and celery and sautee them for 5 minutes
